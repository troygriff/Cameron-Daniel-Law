Energy and Technology Law

About

Cameron-Daniel, P.C. is a California law firm specializing in energy, water, and technology law. Organized as a benefit corporation and backed by over 30 years of industry experience, we provide high-quality advice and creative solutions. We seek to better the environment with our actions and deliver responsive, effective, and knowledgeable legal services.

Services

Energy

We have decades of experience in the California energy markets, including energy product procurement, contract negotiation, and rate-setting.

Water

Our team provides experienced support related to water pumping, waterway restoration, Proposition 218, and hydroelectric generation.

Technology

Cameron-Daniel, P.C. has extensive experience in assisting clients with decisions on emerging technologies, manufacturing operations, and strategic busines operations.

Attorneys





Dan Griffiths

Attorney

Dan serves clients in the electricity and water sectors, providing general counsel and transactional support for new projects, and serving as a legal advocate before appellate courts and regulatory bodies.

Dan received his bachelor’s degree in business economics from the University of California, Santa Barbara and his Juris Doctor degree from the University of California, Davis School of Law. Dan is a member of the California State Bar.




Bruce McLaughlin

Attorney

Bruce has 25 years of experience providing legal services in the energy and technology sectors. Clients have ranged from companies with gross sales in the tens of billions to small businesses with annual gross sales under 5 million. Technologies and industries include aerospace, automotive, forest products, glass products, plastics and composites, energy generation systems, manufacturing processes, machine design and development, software development, waste reduction, ergonomic safety devices and environmental remediation.
